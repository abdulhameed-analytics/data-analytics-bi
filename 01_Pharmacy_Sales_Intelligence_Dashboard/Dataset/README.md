# Pharmacy Sales Intelligence Dashboard

## Project Overview

The Pharmacy Sales Intelligence Dashboard is a Power BI business intelligence solution designed to analyze pharmacy sales performance, product trends, branch performance, and revenue growth.

This dashboard helps stakeholders monitor key business metrics and make data-driven decisions to improve operational efficiency and profitability.

---

## Business Problem

Pharmacy management requires a centralized dashboard to:

* Monitor overall sales performance
* Track revenue and profit trends
* Analyze product category performance
* Evaluate branch-level sales results
* Identify top-performing products
* Support strategic business decisions

---

## Objectives

* Analyze pharmacy sales data
* Measure key business KPIs
* Identify sales trends and patterns
* Compare branch performance
* Improve visibility into business operations

---

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Power Query

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales
* Total Revenue
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin
* Total Transactions
* Average Order Value
* Top Selling Products
* Branch Performance

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Overview

High-level business summary including sales, revenue, profit, and KPI performance.

### Sales Analysis

Detailed analysis of sales trends across different periods.

### Product Performance

Evaluation of product categories and top-performing items.

### Branch Performance

Comparison of branch-level performance and contribution to total sales.
